Hardware - 1992

Below is a list of the hardware configuration of my computer as it was used in the 1992 episodes.

  • Motherboard: Unknown (Modula Tech?), FOREX FRX46C402/FRX36C300-based chipset. 8 ISA-slots, 8x 30-pin SIMM-slots, 64 kB of cache.
  • CPU: Am386DX, 33 MHz. Without NPU.
  • RAM: 5 MB (4x 1MB SIMMs, 4x 256 kB SIMMs.)
  • Video card: ET3000AX based Super VGA board, 512 kB of VRAM.
  • Sound card: Orchid Sound Producer Pro (SoundBlaster Pro clone with Covox Speech Thing and Disney Sound Source support.)
  • Hard disk: Quantum Prodrive LPS 240/AT.
  • Floppy drives: 1x 3.5" drive, 1x 5.25" drive.
  • Modem: Amstrad SM2400, 2400 baud modem.
  • Printer: Commodore MPS 1230, 9-pin dot-matrix printer.
