1992 - A Tour of Windows

A meandering tour of Windows, where I peek into every pre-installed application and control panel setting. The tour ends with a quick montage of all background pictures and screen saver. Exciting.

Afterwards, I install Microsoft Publisher and Microsoft Works and make an unconvincing attempt at using the programs. I also get to show off my 9-pin dot matrix printer and all the wonderful noises it makes.
