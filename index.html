Hello, and welcome to Old Computers sucked! This website belongs to the Youtube channel with the same name, in which I explore the PC and its software through time, year by year. On this website, I've gathered some of the software I've used in the series, along with descriptions and other related information.

Aside from showing the hardware, I also tend to go into the history and background for a lot of the things I show/talk about, as I'm trying to show the hardware and software in a context.
